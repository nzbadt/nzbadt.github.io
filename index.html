About

I am an applied geophysicist and geomechanics specialist transitioning from academia to industry, with expertise in subsurface deformation, reservoir geomechanics, and fault mechanics under coupled stress, temperature, and pore-pressure conditions. My work integrates laboratory experiments, quantitative modeling, and physical intuition to address problems relevant to energy production, carbon storage, and subsurface risk mitigation.
